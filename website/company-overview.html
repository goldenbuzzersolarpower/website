Company Overview

Golden Buzzer Solar Power Engineering Services is a modern and innovative solar solutions provider committed to delivering the highest level of excellence in renewable energy services.

The name “Golden Buzzer” represents the highest standard of performance — a symbol of outstanding quality, reliability, and excellence. Just like the golden buzzer signifies elite talent, our company guarantees elite solar engineering solutions.

Headquartered in Parañaque (Metro Manila), the company specializing in hybrid solar systems for residential properties and select commercial and industrial facilities where power reliability and long-term system performance are critical.

We design and install solar solutions based on actual energy usage, real load behavior, and operational requirements—not estimates, generic templates, or sales-driven assumptions.

We are proud to be a company known for speed, efficiency, and superior workmanship — delivering projects faster without compromising quality.
